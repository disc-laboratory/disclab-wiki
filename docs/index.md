# DISC Lab Wiki

Welcome to the starter knowledge base for the **Digital Intelligence for Structures and Construction (DISC) Lab**.

This site is designed as the lab's central documentation system. It should support:

- research reproducibility
- onboarding of new members
- standardization of workflows
- transfer of knowledge across projects
- clear internal policies and expectations

## How this wiki should be used

This wiki should function as the lab's **single source of operational truth** for recurring procedures, technical workflows, templates, and internal expectations.

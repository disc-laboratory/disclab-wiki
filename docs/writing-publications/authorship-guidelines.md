# Authorship Guidelines

## Principles

Authorship should reflect substantial scholarly contribution.

## Typical contribution areas

- conceptualization
- methodology
- data curation
- software
- validation
- formal analysis
- writing
- supervision
- funding acquisition

## Minimum expectation for co-authorship

A co-author should contribute meaningfully to the scientific content and review the manuscript before submission.

## Recommendation

Discuss likely authorship early, especially for multi-student projects.

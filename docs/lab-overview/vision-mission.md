# Vision and Mission

## Vision

DISC Lab advances the digitalization, monitoring, and automation of infrastructure and construction through the integration of computer vision, machine learning, robotics, and extended reality.

## Mission

The lab aims to develop scientifically rigorous and practically relevant methods for:

- objective condition assessment of structures
- semantic understanding of built environments
- digital representations of assets across scales
- AI-assisted inspection and decision support
- perception-enabled construction automation

## Core values

- **Scientific rigor**: methods must be technically defensible and well validated.
- **Reproducibility**: all major outputs should be traceable and repeatable.
- **Practical relevance**: research should connect to real civil engineering problems.
- **Interdisciplinarity**: combine structural engineering with AI, vision, XR, and robotics.
- **Professional conduct**: documentation, communication, and collaboration must be reliable.

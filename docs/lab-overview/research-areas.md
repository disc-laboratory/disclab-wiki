# Research Areas

## Main pillars

### 1. AI for structural inspection and condition assessment
- damage detection and segmentation
- damage quantification and characterization
- explainable and uncertainty-aware models

### 2. Infrastructure and building digitalization
- photogrammetry and 3D reconstruction
- semantic scene understanding
- asset-scale and city-scale digital representations

### 3. XR for engineering workflows
- near real-time visual inspection
- holographic visualization of engineering information
- human-AI interaction in field inspection tasks

### 4. Robotics and automation in construction
- perception for robotic construction
- semantic understanding for task planning
- monitoring and support for assembly and retrofitting workflows

## Expected output types
- datasets
- trained models
- software tools
- 3D models and digital assets
- journal and conference papers
- demonstrations and prototypes

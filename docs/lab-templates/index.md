# DISCLab Templates

This section collects the main templates used across the DISC Laboratory for documents, reports, presentations, and other research-related materials.

The purpose of these templates is to promote consistency, save time, and ensure that commonly used documents follow a clear and professional structure aligned with the lab’s practices.

## What you will find here

The templates provided in this section may include:

- LaTeX templates for internal and formal documents;
- report and presentation templates;
- reusable formats for research documentation and communication.

## How to use these templates

Each template page contains a short description of the document, together with links to download the source files and, when available, a PDF version for quick reference.

In general:

- use these templates as starting points;
- create a copy before editing;
- avoid modifying the master version directly unless you are updating the official template for the lab.

## General intention

The templates are intended to support efficient and consistent work across the lab. They are not meant to restrict flexibility unnecessarily, but to provide a reliable base that members can adapt to specific needs while maintaining a coherent lab identity.

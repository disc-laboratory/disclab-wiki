# Publication Strategy Roadmap (PhD)

## General principle

Publications should follow a **coherent research trajectory**, not a collection of unrelated outputs.

## Typical PhD trajectory

### Early stage

- literature review;
- baseline implementations;
- initial conference paper (optional).

### Mid stage

- methodological contributions;
- conference papers;
- initial journal submission.

### Final stage

- mature contributions;
- journal papers;
- thesis consolidation.

## Strategy recommendations

- aim for quality over quantity;
- avoid splitting contributions unnecessarily;
- align publications with thesis structure.

## Venue selection

- choose venues based on relevance to the research;
- consider audience and impact;
- balance speed vs rigor.

## Final note

A good publication strategy supports both **scientific contribution and career development**.

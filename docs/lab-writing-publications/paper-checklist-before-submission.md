# Paper Checklist Before Submission

## Purpose

This checklist ensures that the manuscript is ready for submission and meets the lab’s quality standards.

## Content checklist

- the problem and contribution are clearly stated;
- the methodology is well described;
- results support the claims;
- limitations are acknowledged where relevant.

## Figures and tables

- all figures are high quality and readable;
- captions are clear and informative;
- figures are referenced and explained in the text;
- tables are consistent and properly formatted.

## Reproducibility

- dataset is clearly described;
- experimental setup is documented;
- results are traceable to experiments;
- configuration details are available.

## Writing quality

- grammar and language are correct;
- terminology is consistent;
- no redundant or unclear sections;
- logical flow between sections.

## Formatting

- template requirements are followed;
- references are correctly formatted;
- figures and tables are properly placed;
- submission guidelines of the venue are respected.

## Authorship and approvals

- all authors have reviewed the manuscript;
- authorship order is agreed;
- PI has approved submission.

## Final checks

- no missing figures or references;
- no placeholder text remains;
- PDF compiles correctly.

## Final note

Submission should only occur when the paper is **complete, coherent, and validated**.
